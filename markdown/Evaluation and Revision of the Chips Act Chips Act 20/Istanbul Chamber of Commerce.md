As the Istanbul Chamber of Commerce, our views regarding the review process of the European Chips Act are presented below: Türkiye, through the Customs Union framework and its alignment with technical regulations, serves as a reliable supply partner in chemicals and materials used in semiconductor manufacturing. Within programs such as Horizon Europe, Turkish institutions participate in joint R&D projects in semiconductor materials, sensor technologies, and photonics. Türkiyes low-carbon production capacity can further support the EUs sustainability objectives. Moreover, a joint Associated Partner Mechanism should be established with non-EU neighboring countriesparticularly Türkiyefor cooperation in technology and supply chains. Respectfully,
