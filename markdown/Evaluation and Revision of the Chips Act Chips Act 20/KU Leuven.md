- We should not only invest in manufacturing, but also in design and building up the complete ecosystem of fabless companies. We want to have the next Nvidia, not (only?) the next TSMC... - We should invest more in academic research in close (and real) collaboration with EU companies. Do not think that this collaboration automatically comes from investing in large research institutes. They work less with local industry. You need a widely distributed network of industry and researchers, with a lot of mutual exchange of personnel (transferring from one entity to another, e.g. a PhD students then starting a job in a company) and ideas. This is more common between academia and local companies.
