We need the Chip Act to protect European semiconductor production, but it cannot be that the proposal contains not a single word about printed circuit boards, without which the whole undertaking makes no sense. EU legislation should also cover (and protect) this area, as it is directly related to our security and determines the greater or lesser dependence of the EU on third countries.
