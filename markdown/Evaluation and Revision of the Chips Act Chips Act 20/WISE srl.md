As a European manufacturer of equipment for printed circuit board (PCB) production, I strongly support the objectives of the Chips Act 2.0. Strengthening Europes semiconductor ecosystem requires attention to the entire value chain, including PCB technologies and the machinery that enables advanced packaging and electronic assembly. We encourage the Commission to: Recognize equipment manufacturing as a strategic pillar of semiconductor sovereignty; Include SMEs and specialized European suppliers in funding and cooperation programs; Support innovation and sustainability in production equipment (energy efficiency, eco-friendly processes); Simplify investment and permitting procedures to accelerate capacity build-up. By integrating PCB and equipment makers into the Chips Act 2.0, Europe can secure a truly resilient, innovative, and competitive semiconductor ecosystem.
